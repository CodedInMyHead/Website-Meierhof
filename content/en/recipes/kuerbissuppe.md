## Ingredients

- 1 kg Hokkaido pumpkin
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger
- 400 ml coconut milk
- 500 ml vegetable stock
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt, pepper, nutmeg
- Pumpkin seed oil and pumpkin seeds to garnish

## Directions

1. Wash the pumpkin, remove the seeds and cut into rough chunks.
2. Peel and finely chop the onion, garlic and ginger.
3. Heat olive oil and sauté the onion until translucent.
4. Add garlic and ginger, then add the pumpkin.
5. Pour in the stock and coconut milk, simmer for 20 minutes.
6. Blend until smooth and season with salt, pepper and nutmeg.
7. Serve garnished with pumpkin seed oil and pumpkin seeds.

## Notes

Hokkaido pumpkin does not need to be peeled – the skin softens during cooking. For a deeper flavour, roast the pumpkin in the oven first.
