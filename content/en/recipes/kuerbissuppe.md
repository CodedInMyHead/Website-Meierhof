A warming autumn soup with the sweet, nutty aroma of our fresh Hokkaido pumpkin – a treat on cool days.

## Ingredients

- 1 kg Hokkaido pumpkin (skin can be left on)
- 1 onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger
- 400 ml coconut milk
- 500 ml vegetable stock
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt, pepper, nutmeg
- Pumpkin seed oil and pumpkin seeds to garnish

## Instructions

1. Wash the pumpkin, remove the seeds and cut into rough chunks (skin stays on).
2. Peel and finely chop the onion, garlic and ginger.
3. Heat olive oil in a large pot and sauté the onion until translucent.
4. Add garlic and ginger and cook briefly, then add the pumpkin.
5. Pour in the vegetable stock and coconut milk, bring to a boil and simmer for 20 minutes.
6. Blend the soup until smooth and season with salt, pepper and nutmeg.
7. Serve in bowls and garnish with pumpkin seed oil and pumpkin seeds.
