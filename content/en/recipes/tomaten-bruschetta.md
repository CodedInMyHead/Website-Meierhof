## Ingredients

- 1 baguette
- 500 g ripe tomatoes
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 bunch fresh basil
- 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- Salt, pepper
- 1 tsp balsamic vinegar (optional)

## Directions

1. Slice the baguette and toast in the oven at 200 °C for about 5 minutes.
2. Wash the tomatoes, remove the seeds and dice finely.
3. Roughly chop the basil and finely chop the garlic.
4. Mix tomatoes, basil, garlic and olive oil. Season with salt, pepper and balsamic vinegar.
5. Spoon onto the warm bread slices and serve immediately.

## Notes

Ripe, flavourful tomatoes are the heart of this dish. Bruschetta tastes best in midsummer when tomatoes have ripened in the sun.
