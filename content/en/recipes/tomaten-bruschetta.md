---
title: Tomato Bruschetta
description: Crispy baguette with fresh tomatoes, garlic and basil – quick, simple and irresistible.
---

A summery classic that puts our fresh farm tomatoes in the spotlight. Perfect as a starter or snack.

## Ingredients

- 1 baguette
- 500 g ripe tomatoes
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 bunch fresh basil
- 4 tbsp olive oil (extra virgin)
- Salt, pepper
- 1 tsp balsamic vinegar (optional)

## Instructions

1. Slice the baguette and toast in the oven at 200 °C for about 5 minutes until golden and crispy.
2. Wash the tomatoes, remove the seeds and dice finely.
3. Roughly chop the basil and finely chop or press the garlic.
4. Mix tomatoes, basil, garlic and olive oil together. Season with salt, pepper and balsamic vinegar.
5. Spoon the tomato mixture onto the warm bread slices and serve immediately.
