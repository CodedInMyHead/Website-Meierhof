title: Asparagus with Hollandaise Sauce

The ultimate classic – fresh white asparagus from Meierhof, served with a silky, buttery Hollandaise sauce.

## Ingredients

- 1 kg white asparagus
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp butter

**For the Hollandaise sauce:**

- 3 egg yolks
- 150 g clarified butter
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt, white pepper

## Instructions

1. Peel the asparagus and cut off the woody ends.
2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil with salt, sugar and butter.
3. Add the asparagus and cook for 12–18 minutes until tender but still firm.
4. For the Hollandaise, whisk egg yolks with lemon juice over a bain-marie until creamy.
5. Slowly whisk in the clarified butter until a thick sauce forms.
6. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately with the asparagus.
