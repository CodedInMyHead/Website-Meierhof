title: Asparagus with Hollandaise Sauce
servings: 4
time: 35 min
difficulty: 3

---

## Ingredients

- 1 kg white asparagus
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 150 g clarified butter
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the asparagus and cut off the woody ends.
2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil with salt, sugar and butter.
3. Add the asparagus and cook for 12–18 minutes until tender but firm.
4. For the Hollandaise, whisk egg yolks with lemon juice over a bain-marie until creamy.
5. Slowly whisk in the clarified butter until a thick sauce forms.
6. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.

## Notes

White asparagus from Meierhof is freshly cut daily – best used the same day. The Hollandaise should be prepared just before serving as it does not reheat well.
