# Our Story

Meierhof Staffort has been a family farm for over four generations. What began as a small market garden has grown into a diverse family farm in the heart of the fertile fields of Stutensee-Staffort.

Our passion is seasonal quality: from April to June, asparagus takes centre stage — we grow both white and green asparagus, harvested fresh every day. Summer brings fragrant strawberries, followed by pumpkins, apples, and autumn vegetables later in the year.

We believe in bringing good, regional food to the people around us. Come and visit — our farm shop looks forward to welcoming you.
